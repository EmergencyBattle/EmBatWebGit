PORTHOS

Porthos is a concept for a smart jacket aimed at trying to help locate and rescue people in an outdoors environment with a particular focus on avalanche situations. The concept had to somehow include wireless communication and the theme our group was assigned was "health issues prevention".

#FLOD

#FLOD was a class project in which we had to make an interactive prototype on the theme of religion. In Swedish, the Christian myth of the Great Flood is called "syndafloden", roughly translated "the flood of sin". As such we decided to make a bottle ship where the bottle was rocked to create waves whenever certain negative words were posted on twitter. When you then placed your hand on the shaking bottle, the shaking would stop as you "calmed" the bottle. The first video is a video prototype where most of the interaction has been faked to convey the concept, and the second video shows the bottle actually moving.
